Hello all,

I'm proud to announce immediate availablity of supporting modules for
TableLayout and FormLayout layout managers.
NetBeans 3.6 and NetBeans 4.0 are supported.

Modules available for download from
https://developer.berlios.de/projects/nblayoutpack/

TableLayout supporting module changes:
- Fixed bug with JDK 1.5 NullPointerException on layout customizer 
activation.

FormLayout supporting module changes:
- New groups custom editor;
- Move up/Move down actions in column/row editor.
